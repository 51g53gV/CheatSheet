Nmap scan
===========================================================================================================================================================================================================
->Discovery Scan
	nmap 192.168.1.1<-----------------------//Scan for one IP
	nmap 192.168.1.0-20<--------------------//Scan a range of IPs (192.168.0.0-192.168.0.20)
	nmap 192.168.1.1/24<--------------------//Scan a subnet (192.168.0.0 subnet 255.255.255.0)
	nmap -T4 192.168.1.1<-------------------//Fast basic scan
	nmap -sn 192.168.1.1<-------------------//Ping scan without port scanning

->Service Scanning
	nmap -sS 192.168.1.1<-------------------//SYN Stealth scan of services
	nmap -sS -sV 192.168.1.1<---------------//SYN Stealth scan of version
	nmap -Pn 192.168.1.1<-------------------//Ping scan with port scanning

->Port scan
	nmap -p 22 192.168.1.1<-----------------//Scan a signle port(22)
	nmap -p 1-100 192.168.1.1<--------------//Scan a range of port(1-100)
	nmap -F 192.168.1.1<--------------------//Scan 100 most common ports
	nmap -p- 192.168.1.1<-------------------//Scan all 65535 ports

->Android Scan
	nmap -sn 192.168.1.1<-------------------//Ping scan without port scanning/Discover Android devices
	nmap -Pn 192.168.1.1<-------------------//
